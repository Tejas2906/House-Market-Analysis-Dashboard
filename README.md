# House Market Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **House Market Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to explore real estate trends, pricing behavior, and sales performance across regions and house types.  
This project was created as part of my **learning and hands-on practice with Power BI**, focusing on data modeling, DAX measures, and analytical dashboard design.

---

## Project Purpose

The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze **housing market trends** across regions
- Compare **offer price vs purchase price**
- Understand price variation by **house type**
- Track **sales performance and growth patterns**
- Practice building multi-page Power BI reports

---

## Report Pages Overview

The Power BI report contains **3 pages**, each designed to answer specific business questions.

---

### Page 1: House Market Overview
![House Market Overview](Images/Page1_House_Market_Overview.png)

**Key Insights:**
- Median sales price varies significantly across regions
- Some regions show **positive price growth**, while others decline
- Strong correlation between **offer price and purchase price**
- Total 12-month sales value shows market scale and activity

---

### Page 2: House Type & Price Analysis
![House Type Analysis](Images/Page2_House_Type_Analysis.png)

**Key Insights:**
- **Farm and Apartment** properties have higher average prices
- Villas and Summerhouses show lower purchase values
- Price per square meter differs notably by house type
- Inflation, interest, and yield vary across property categories

---

### Page 3: Sales Performance Analysis
![Sales Performance](Images/Page3_Sales_Performance.png)

**Key Insights:**
- Zealand and Jutland contribute the highest sales volume
- Regular sales outperform auction and family sales
- Key influencer analysis highlights factors increasing purchase price
- Regional price per SQM shows uneven market demand

  - Data cleaning and transformation
